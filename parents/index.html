PARENT FAQ

Good questions, honest answers

Does the Parent Hub track or grade my child?

No. There are no grades or labels — skills show as growth-framed status like Strong, Growing, or Let's practice, and you see activity highlights, not raw scores.

Is the Parent Hub free? Do I have to set it up?

It's free, optional, and never blocks play. Setup takes under a minute from the app's settings.

What's in the weekly email?

Activity highlights — stars earned, problems solved, highest level — and a short summary of the week. Nothing sensitive, ever.

How do I stop the emails?

One click on the unsubscribe link in any email, anytime.

What ages is Math Magic for?

It's built for elementary and middle school math — number sense, fractions, decimals, and long division — designed for ages 6–12.
